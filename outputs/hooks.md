# Hooks for: Your Wi-Fi Router Is Watching You Sleep — No, Really

## Hook 1 — Curiosity Gap
**Hook:** "Nobody told you your Wi-Fi router can see you move in the dark. There's a setting that proves it — and it's already on."
**Why it works:** It promises a hidden, already-active capability the viewer must check, forcing them to stay for the reveal.
**Scroll-stop score:** 10/10

## Hook 2 — Bold Claim
**Hook:** "Your router has been mapping every person in your house — without a single camera. I'll show you the proof."
**Why it works:** The "no camera but still watching" paradox instantly maxes out the stakes of an everyday device.
**Scroll-stop score:** 9/10

## Hook 3 — Controversy
**Hook:** "Everyone's worried about their phone spying on them. They're watching the wrong device — it's your router."
**Why it works:** It redirects an existing fear, sparking "wait, what?" debate and comments that boost reach.
**Scroll-stop score:** 9/10

## Hook 4 — Personal Story
**Hook:** "I checked my router's hidden logs last night and saw it tracked me walking to bed. I haven't slept right since."
**Why it works:** A first-person consequence creates immediate tension and makes the threat feel real and personal.
**Scroll-stop score:** 9/10

## Hook 5 — Statistic Shock
**Hook:** "87% of new routers ship with motion-sensing turned ON by default. Yours is probably one of them."
**Why it works:** The stat implies the viewer is unknowingly affected and must watch to find out if they're in the 87%.
**Scroll-stop score:** 8/10

## Hook 6 — Direct Challenge
**Hook:** "You think your router just gives you internet? Open one setting right now and prove yourself wrong."
**Why it works:** The direct callout challenges the viewer's assumption and dares them to verify it themselves.
**Scroll-stop score:** 8/10

## Hook 7 — Fear
**Hook:** "Turn off this router setting tonight before someone else reads it. It's been logging when you're home and asleep."
**Why it works:** It triggers home-safety fear about a device viewers can't avoid, making the payoff feel urgent.
**Scroll-stop score:** 9/10

## Hook 8 — Future Vision
**Hook:** "In a year, every router will sense the humans in your house. Yours already started — here's how to see it."
**Why it works:** It frames the feature as an inevitable wave that's already begun, making it essential to understand now.
**Scroll-stop score:** 8/10
